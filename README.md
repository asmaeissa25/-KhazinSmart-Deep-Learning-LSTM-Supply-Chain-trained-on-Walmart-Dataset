## ðŸ§  The AI Engine: Deep Learning (LSTM)
The core of KhazinSmart isn't just a dashboard; it's powered by a **Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM)** recurrent neural network.

* **Global-Scale Training:** The model is trained on the **Walmart Sales Dataset**, processing millions of historical transactions to recognize complex retail patterns.
* **Time-Series Precision:** Analyzes seasonality, promotional impacts, and price elasticity to forecast demand for the next 7-30 days.
* **Moroccan Context:** Optimized to handle local market fluctuations and supply chain dynamics in cities like Casablanca, Tangier, and Agadir.
